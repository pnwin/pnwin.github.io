Minecraft

A simple Minecraft-to-Real world 3D print. Created with the use of the popular sandbox game in tandem with Mineways (a world editor). The design was inspired by the EMO Robot, a desktop "pet" that has the functionalies of Alex, and the personality of an actual pet.
First sculpted on the school's minecraft server, it was sculpted with care and implemented with the smallest minute details.
The sculpt was then exported and printed on a PLA printer as the draft.
The final result was adjusted in-game, then printed on a resin printer.
