Net Art

 
Net art was a project in which I was able to create a whole digital story on the internet, entirely through hand-coding. One of the more important functions is the story tree, in which your decisions branch off into another portion of the story, which features two distincive paths to one ending. This story tells about a somewhat unhygeinic balloon and the problems that entail when he forgets to do one simple avoidable task...
